Our Story

ToLiveSober was born from a simple idea: sobriety doesn't mean the end of a vibrant life—it's often the beginning of one. Based in Austin, Texas, we're passionate about showcasing the rich, fulfilling experiences available to those embracing an alcohol-free lifestyle.

Our community celebrates the journey of recovery while providing practical resources, authentic stories, and local connections for anyone navigating sobriety in the Lone Star State and beyond.

Whether you're newly sober, sober-curious, or supporting someone on their journey, you've found a welcoming space that honors every step of the path to clarity.
